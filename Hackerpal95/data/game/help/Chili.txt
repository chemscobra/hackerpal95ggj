Best Chili con Carne in town

Ingredients
1      Large Onion
1      Tomato
1 cube Pork Stock
5sp    Cumin
5sp    Pepper
3sp    Salt
200g   Red Beans
500g   Minced Meat
400g   Tomato Sauce

Preparation

In a large pot, sautée the already salted and finely chopped onions until they become slightly transparent, add the meat, the cumin and the pepper and sautée until cooked. Mash the stock cube and make sure it covers the meat evenly.

Add both the tomato sauce and the chopped tomato, mix thoroughly and let simmer for 30 minutes. Add the beans and allow to simmer for 10 additional minutes.

Serve hot.
